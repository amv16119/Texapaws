OUR MISSION

There are millions of dogs and cats with no safety net. They are euthanized when their owners can’t afford a necessary medical procedure. We see a better way. Imagine the pain of losing your beloved companion because you couldn’t afford vital treatment. We see a better way. We are building the world’s first safety net for dogs and cats in a medical crisis.

Right now, there are dogs and cats who need a hero. Will you be theirs?

Our thousands of donors from around the globe commit to making a small monthly donation to The Waggle Foundation. Donors select an amount that is comfortable for them, and over time, these contributions become a sustainable and powerful source of funding for the pets who need it the most. These pledged contributions allow us to expend all the resources we can towards saving as many lives as possible: Through a recurring donation—even a modest one—you can save and change lives.
